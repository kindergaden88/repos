Electronic product registration

When you buy and install a new product, we may ask you to register your purchase electronically. When you do this, we will combine your registration data with any information that you have already left us (we call this information your personal profile). If you have not previously been registered with us, we will create your personal profile based on your product registration data. If you ever want to review or update this information, you can visit the Profile Center, click on the update profile and edit any personal information in your profile. If you have not created a registration ID, we ask you to do so. This ensures that only you can access your information.

Customer profiles

As mentioned above, each registered user has a unique personal profile. Each profile is assigned a unique identification number, which allows us to guarantee that only you can access your profile.

When you register, we create your profile, assign a personal identification number, and then send this personal identification number to your hard drive in the form of a cookie, which is a small piece of code. This code is your unique. This is your passport for an unhindered trip to www.it-ments.com, which allows you to download free software, order free newsletters, and visit premium sites without having to fill out registration forms with the information that you have already provided. Even if you change computers, you do not have to re-register - just use the registration ID to identify yourself.
